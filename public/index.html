Instructions
  • 1) Open your hands in front of the camera and press the up button until you get 20 or more images.
  • 2) Close your hands in front of the camera and press the down button until you get 20 or more images.
  • 3) Click on train
  • 4) When it says Done Training, click on prepare
  • 5) Click play!

The idea is to catch all the incoming coins. Open your fist to move the ball up, and make a fist to move the ball down.
